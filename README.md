# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis (2015)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes pizza sales data from 2015 using Power BI to uncover business insights, customer behavior, and sales trends. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track key business KPIs
- Identify sales trends and seasonal patterns
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate product and category performance
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 📌 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** $817.86K  
- **Total Orders:** 21K  
- **Total Pizzas Sold:** 50K  
- **Average Order Value:** $38.31  
- **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.32  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🕒 Sales Trends
- Peak sales occur in **mid-year (July)**  
- Lower performance in **February, September, October, and December**  
- Weekly sales range from **$13K – $17K**

---

### 📅 Customer Behavior
- **61% of orders contain multiple items**, indicating strong upselling opportunities  
- Peak hours:
  - Lunch: **12 PM – 2 PM**
  - Dinner: **5 PM – 7 PM**
- Best-performing days: **Thursday – Saturday**

---

### 🍕 Product Performance
- **Classic category** leads in both revenue and quantity  
- Large (L) size dominates sales  
- Top ingredients: Garlic, Tomatoes, Red Onions  
- Low-performing ingredients: Brie Carre, Thyme, Prosciutto  

---

### 💰 Pricing Insights
- Strong negative correlation between price and demand (**r = -0.95**)  
- Lower-priced categories achieve higher sales volume  

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations
- Introduce **bundle deals and combo offers** to increase order value  
- Focus on promoting **top-performing products**  
- Optimize or remove **low-performing items**  
- Run promotions during **low-demand months**  
- Adjust pricing strategy to balance volume and profitability
